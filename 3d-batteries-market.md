<p><p>The 3D batteries market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Interdigitated Structure** - This design increases surface area by alternating electrodes, enhancing ion flow and energy density.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Concentric Structure** - This configuration stacks electrodes concentrically, optimizing volume and promoting efficient ion movement.</p><p>3. **Aperiodic Structure** - This irregular layout maximizes space utilization, allowing for complex ion pathways and improved conductivity, making it suitable for advanced applications.</p><p>Each structure offers distinct advantages in energy efficiency and performance, driving innovation in battery technology.</p></p>
