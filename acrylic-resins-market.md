<p><ul><li>Methacrylates</li><li>Acrylates</li><li>Hybrids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic resins market is primarily segmented into three types: methacrylates, acrylates, and hybrids. Methacrylates, derived from methacrylic acid, are known for their excellent clarity and durability, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. Acrylates are versatile and widely used in paints, sealants, and textiles due to their strong adhesion properties. Hybrid resins combine both methacrylates and acrylates, offering enhanced performance characteristics. Together, these types cater to diverse applications across automotive, construction, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
