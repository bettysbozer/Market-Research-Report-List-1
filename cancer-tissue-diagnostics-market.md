<p><ul><li>Immunohistochemistry (IHC) Test</li><li>In Situ Hybridisation Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cancer Tissue Diagnostics market encompasses various testing methodologies used to analyze cancerous tissues. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) tests detect specific antigens in tissue sections, providing insights into protein expression patterns associated with different cancer types. In Situ Hybridisation (ISH) tests, on the other hand, identify specific nucleic acid sequences within the tissue, helping to characterize genetic abnormalities or gene expression. Both methods play crucial roles in aiding diagnosis, determining treatment strategies, and guiding personalized medicine approaches for cancer patients.</p></p>
