<p><p>The captive fastener market includes various types such as screws, nuts, and rivets, each designed for specific applications. Screws provide secure, adjustable fastening solutions and can be easily installed or removed. Nuts complement bolts or screws to create strong joints, often in conjunction with washers for added stability. Rivets are permanent fasteners that join materials by deforming one end, offering durability and strength in applications where vibration resistance is crucial. Together, these fasteners enhance assembly efficiency and structural integrity.</p></p>
