<p><ul><li>Water-Free Cell Thawing System</li><li>Water Bath Cell Thawing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cell Thawing System market is categorized into two main types: Water-Free Cell Thawing Systems and Water Bath Cell Thawing Systems. Water-Free Cell Thawing Systems utilize advanced technologies such as air convection or conduction methods to thaw cells without water, minimizing contamination risks and providing precise temperature control. In contrast, Water Bath Cell Thawing Systems rely on submerging cryopreserved samples in heated water baths, ensuring a uniform thawing process but potentially increasing the risk of cross-contamination. Both systems are essential for effective cell recovery in various applications.</p></p>
