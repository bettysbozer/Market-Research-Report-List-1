<p><p>Change-over switches are critical devices used to switch between different power sources or circuits seamlessly. The market for these switches is primarily divided into two types: automatic and manual. Automatic change-over switches operate without human intervention, switching power sources based on pre-set conditions such as power failure, ensuring uninterrupted supply. In contrast, manual change-over switches require a user to physically switch the circuit, offering greater control but less convenience. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, depending on operational needs.</p></p>
