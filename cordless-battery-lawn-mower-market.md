<p><p>The cordless battery lawn mower market comprises two main types: self-propelled and push mowers. Self-propelled mowers feature an integrated drive system, allowing the mower to move forward automatically, reducing the physical effort required by the user. They are ideal for larger lawns or uneven terrain. Push mowers, on the other hand, require the user to manually propel the mower forward, offering more control and maneuverability, making them suitable for smaller, well-maintained lawns. Both types provide the convenience of cordless operation and are environmentally friendly.</p></p>
