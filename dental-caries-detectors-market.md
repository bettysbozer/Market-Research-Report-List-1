<p><ul><li>Laser Fluorescent Caries Detector</li><li>Fiber Optic Trans-illumination Caries Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental caries detectors market comprises advanced diagnostic tools that enhance the detection of dental caries. Laser fluorescent caries detectors utilize laser technology to identify carious lesions by measuring fluorescence levels emitted by tooth enamel. In contrast, fiber optic trans-illumination caries detectors use fiber optics to transmit light through teeth, revealing carious areas as darker spots against the illuminated enamel. Both types are increasingly popular for their ability to improve early detection and treatment outcomes in dental practice.</p></p>
