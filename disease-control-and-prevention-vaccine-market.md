<p><p>The Disease Control and Prevention Vaccine Market consists of various types: </p><p>1. **Live-Attenuated Vaccines** use weakened forms of pathogens to promote immunity without causing disease. </p><p>2. **Inactivated Vaccines** contain killed pathogens, stimulating an immune response without any risk of infection. </p><p>3. **Recombinant Subunit Vaccines** include specific pieces of pathogens, such as proteins, to elicit a targeted immune response. </p><p>4. **Toxoid Vaccines** are made from inactivated toxins and help the body build immunity against toxin-producing bacteria. These diverse modalities enhance public health by providing effective disease prevention strategies.</p></p>
