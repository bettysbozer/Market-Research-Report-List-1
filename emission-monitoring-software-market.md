<p><p>Emission Monitoring Software encompasses various solutions to track and analyze pollution outputs from industrial processes. Continuous Emission Monitoring Software (CEMS) provides real-time data on emissions, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations by continuously tracking pollutants. In contrast, Predictive Emission Monitoring Software (PEMS) utilizes algorithms and historical data to forecast emission levels, allowing organizations to anticipate regulatory requirements and optimize operations. Both types of software play a critical role in managing environmental impact effectively and enhancing sustainability in industrial sectors.</p></p>
