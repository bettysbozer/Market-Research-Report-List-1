<p><p>The FVMQ rubber market is categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and semisolid FVMQ. Liquid FVMQ is typically used for applications requiring high viscosity and flexibility, ideal for coatings and sealants. Solid FVMQ, known for its excellent mechanical properties, is utilized in high-performance applications such as automotive and aerospace components. Semisolid FVMQ offers a balance between the two, providing versatility in processing and application, making it suitable for diverse industrial uses such as adhesives and elastomers.</p></p>
