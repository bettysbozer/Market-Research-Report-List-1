<p><p>IC trays, essential for packaging and transporting integrated circuits, are classified into several market types based on material composition. MPPE (Modified Polyphenylene Ether) offers durability and chemical resistance. PES (Polyethersulfone) is known for its thermal stability and high mechanical strength. PS (Polystyrene) and ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) provide lightweight and cost-effective solutions. Other materials may include polycarbonate and polypropylene, catering to specific requirements such as enhanced protection, anti-static properties, and varying thicknesses to meet diverse industry needs.</p></p>
