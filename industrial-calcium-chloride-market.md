<p><p>The industrial calcium chloride market is primarily divided into two types: granular and powdery forms. Granular industrial calcium chloride consists of larger, coarse particles, making it suitable for applications such as dust control and road deicing, where a slower dissolution rate is beneficial. In contrast, powdery industrial calcium chloride features fine particles, offering faster dissolution, ideal for applications requiring quick absorption, such as in food processing or as a desiccant. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs and specific application requirements.</p></p>
