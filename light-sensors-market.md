<p><ul><li>Contact Light Sensor</li><li>Non-Contact Light Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light sensor market is categorized into two main types: contact light sensors and non-contact light sensors. Contact light sensors require direct contact with the light source to measure intensity, commonly used in applications like photography and environmental monitoring. In contrast, non-contact light sensors detect light levels from a distance, utilizing technologies such as photodiodes and phototransistors, which are prevalent in smart devices, automation, and navigation systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency and automation across various industries.</p></p>
