<p><ul><li>Electromagnet System</li><li>Permanent Magnet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic clamping systems primarily fall into two categories: electromagnet systems and permanent magnet systems. Electromagnet systems utilize electricity to generate magnetic fields, allowing for adjustable clamping forces and quick release, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent setups. Conversely, permanent magnet systems use magnetized materials to maintain a constant clamping force without needing power, offering reliability and simplicity in operation. Both systems enhance productivity in manufacturing by securely holding workpieces during machining and assembly processes.</p></p>
