<p><p>The medical device pneumatic cylinders market is categorized into three main types: single-acting cylinders, double-acting cylinders, and others. Single-acting cylinders utilize air pressure for movement in one direction only, often returning to the original position via a spring mechanism. Double-acting cylinders operate in both directions, providing greater power and stability by utilizing air pressure for both extension and retraction. Other types include specialized designs for unique medical applications, enhancing functionality and precision in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
