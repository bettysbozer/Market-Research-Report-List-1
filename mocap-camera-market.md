<p><p>The Mocap (motion capture) camera market is divided into several types: </p><p>1. **Optical-type:** This utilizes multiple cameras to track reflective markers on subjects, providing high accuracy and detail, often used in film and video game production.</p><p>2. **Inertial-type:** These cameras rely on accelerometers and gyroscopes to capture movement without the need for external markers, making them portable and easier to set up for various applications, including sports and virtual reality.</p><p>3. **Other:** This category encompasses various emerging technologies and hybrid systems that might combine optical and inertial methods or utilize depth-sensing technologies for unique mocap solutions.</p></p>
