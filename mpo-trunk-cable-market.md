<p><p>The MPO trunk cable market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mode (SM) and Multimode (MM). Single Mode cables utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal loss, making them ideal for high-speed networks. Multimode cables, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distance connections with larger core diameters, suitable for data centers and local networks. Each type serves distinct applications based on transmission distance and bandwidth requirements.</p></p>
