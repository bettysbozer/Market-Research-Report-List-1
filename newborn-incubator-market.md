<p><p>The newborn incubator market primarily comprises two types: transport baby incubators and normal baby incubators. Transport baby incubators are designed for safe and efficient transport of premature or ill infants, featuring lightweight, portable designs and easy integration with emergency medical vehicles. In contrast, normal baby incubators provide a controlled environment within hospitals, ensuring stability in temperature, humidity, and oxygen levels for newborns requiring prolonged care. Both types are crucial for enhancing neonatal outcomes and supporting vulnerable infants.</p></p>
