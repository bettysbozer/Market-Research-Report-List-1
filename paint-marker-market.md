<p><p>The paint marker market is divided into two primary types: liquid and solid. Liquid paint markers contain ink that flows smoothly, allowing for vibrant, consistent marking on various surfaces. They are popular for detailed work and have refillable options. In contrast, solid paint markers use a crayon-like core, providing a more controlled application and durability, suitable for rough surfaces. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from artists and crafters to industrial applications, offering versatility in marking solutions.</p></p>
