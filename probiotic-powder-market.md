<p><p>The probiotic powder market is primarily categorized into three types: Bifidobacterium, Lactobacillus, and Other. Bifidobacterium, often found in the intestines, aids digestion and supports immune health. Lactobacillus, prevalent in fermented foods, is known for enhancing gut flora and improving lactose digestion. The 'Other' category includes various probiotic strains not classified under the first two, such as Saccharomyces or Streptococcus, which offer diverse health benefits. Together, these types cater to a wide range of consumer needs in gut health.</p></p>
