<p><p>The RGB-IR camera market is segmented into two main types: Line Scan Cameras and Area Scan Cameras. Line Scan Cameras capture images by exposing a single line of pixels at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring high-speed imaging in industrial settings. In contrast, Area Scan Cameras capture the entire frame simultaneously, offering high-resolution images suited for a variety of applications, including surveillance and remote sensing. Both types leverage RGB-IR technology to enhance imaging capabilities in diverse environments.</p></p>
