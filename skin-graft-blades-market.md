<p><ul><li>Full-Thickness Skin Grafts</li><li>Split-Thickness Skin Grafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin graft blades market primarily includes two types: full-thickness skin grafts and split-thickness skin grafts. Full-thickness skin grafts encompass both the epidermis and the entire dermis, making them suitable for aesthetically sensitive areas and providing better healing and cosmetic outcomes. In contrast, split-thickness skin grafts comprise only the epidermis and a portion of the dermis, allowing for larger areas to be covered and more rapid healing. Each type serves specific clinical needs in wound care and reconstructive surgery.</p></p>
