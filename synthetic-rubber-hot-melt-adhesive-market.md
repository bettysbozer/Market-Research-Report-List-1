<p><p>The Synthetic Rubber Hot Melt Adhesive market features two primary types: High-Temperature Vulcanizing (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV). HTV adhesives cure upon heating, providing enhanced thermal resistance and bonding strength, ideal for high-stress applications. In contrast, RTV adhesives cure at room temperature, offering convenience and versatility for various substrates without the need for heating. Both types serve distinct applications across industries such as automotive, packaging, and construction, catering to specific performance requirements and processing conditions.</p></p>
