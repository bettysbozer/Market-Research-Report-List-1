<p><p>The textile fiber dyes market includes various dye types, such as Vinylsulphone Dyes (VS), Monochlorotriazine Dyes (MCT), and Bi-functional Dyes. Vinylsulphone Dyes are reactive dyes known for their bright colors and strong fixation on cotton and other fibers. Monochlorotriazine Dyes are versatile, primarily used for dyeing cellulose fibers with excellent wash fastness. Bi-functional Dyes combine features of both VS and MCT dyes, enabling improved dyeing properties and application flexibility across various fiber types for enhanced performance.</p></p>
