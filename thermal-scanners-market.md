<p><strong>The Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LWIR</li><li>MWIR</li><li>SWIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal scanners are categorized into three main types based on their wavelength ranges: LWIR (Long-Wave Infrared), MWIR (Mid-Wave Infrared), and SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared). LWIR scanners operate between 8-14 micrometers, ideal for detecting heat emissions in various applications, such as building inspections and surveillance. MWIR scanners, ranging from 3-5 micrometers, are used for military and industrial monitoring. SWIR scanners operate from 0.9-1.7 micrometers, suitable for imaging through obscurants like smoke and fog, enhancing visibility in challenging conditions.</p></p>
