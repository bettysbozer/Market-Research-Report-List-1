<p><strong>The Turbinado Sugar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rough Processing</li><li>Fine Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turbinado sugar is categorized into two primary market types based on processing methods: rough processing and fine processing. Rough processing involves minimal refining, resulting in a coarser texture and a light golden-brown color, often preserving more of the natural molasses flavor. In contrast, fine processing entails further refinement, producing a lighter, more uniform granule with a milder taste. Both types are utilized in various applications, including baking, beverages, and cooking, catering to different consumer preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>
