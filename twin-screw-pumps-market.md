<p><p>The Twin Screw Pumps market is segmented into two main types: sealed and non-sealed twin screw pumps. Sealed twin screw pumps feature a hermetic design that minimizes leakage, ensuring safe handling of volatile or hazardous fluids, making them ideal for oil and gas, chemical, and food industries. Conversely, non-sealed twin screw pumps lack this containment, allowing for easier maintenance and lower initial costs, often utilized in applications where leakage is less of a concern, such as wastewater management or bulk transfer.</p></p>
