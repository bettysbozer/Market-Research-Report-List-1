<p><p>The vial washing machine market is primarily categorized into two types: linear tunnel type and rotary type. </p><p>Linear tunnel type machines operate by moving vials through a linear series of cleaning stations, allowing for continuous washing and rinsing. In contrast, rotary type machines utilize a rotating mechanism to wash vials, enabling effective cleaning as vials are turned and exposed to cleaning agents. Both types cater to pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries, ensuring high standards of cleanliness for vials prior to filling.</p></p>
