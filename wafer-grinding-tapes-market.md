<p><p>Wafer grinding tapes are crucial in the semiconductor industry for holding wafers during grinding processes. The UV type uses ultraviolet light-sensitive adhesives, allowing for easy removal after grinding through exposure to UV light, minimizing residue. Non-UV types rely on traditional adhesive methods and do not require light for removal, making them straightforward but potentially more challenging to clean. Both types serve distinct needs based on the specific requirements of wafer handling and surface integrity during manufacturing processes.</p></p>
